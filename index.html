Wings of Fire: The New World - Admin Handbook

Admin Handbook

Created by the admin team for the admin team of Wings of Fire: The New World.

This comprehensive document has been collaboratively authored by multiple members of the administrator team of Wings of Fire: The New World. Its primary objective is to establish a centralized and efficient database encompassing a wide array of essential documentation and pertinent information. The overarching goal behind this endeavor is to facilitate swift, convenient, and comprehensible access to critical insights for our internal staff team.

By consolidating various vital resources within this single repository, we aspire to streamline the process of retrieving pertinent information. This compilation not only ensures the availability of crucial data but also strives to present it in a format that is both easily navigable and readily understandable.
